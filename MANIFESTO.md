\# Project Spatial



\## Manifesto



\### Why Spatial Exists



Project Spatial was created to explore patterns, not to predict the future.



We believe that historical data contains structures that can be discovered through different spatial perspectives.



Our mission is to provide a platform where curiosity becomes experiments, experiments become evidence, and evidence becomes knowledge.



We value reproducibility over assumptions.



We value transparency over promises.



We value understanding over certainty.



Project Spatial is built for explorers, analysts, researchers and everyone who enjoys discovering patterns.



We believe software should not replace thinking.



It should empower it.



\---



\## Our Principles



• Specification before implementation.



• Architecture before code.



• Knowledge before features.



• Reproducibility before conclusions.



• Simplicity before complexity.



• Community before ego.



\---



\## Our Promise



Every feature should have a purpose.



Every experiment should be reproducible.



Every architectural decision should improve the platform.



Every contribution should make Spatial better than it was yesterday.
